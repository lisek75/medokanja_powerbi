Analyzing patient waitlists to optimize healthcare resources and minimize delays.

The Medokanja project focuses on analyzing healthcare waiting lists to enhance resource management and improve patient care for a public hospital. The name "Medokanja" combines "Medo" (medical) and "Kanja" (patient in Japanese).
